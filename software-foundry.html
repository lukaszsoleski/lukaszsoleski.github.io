01 / Observe

Map the operating surface

Before code, I trace how data, users, and external systems move through the business. The goal is to understand what must stay stable and where the friction actually lives.

  • Business decisions and operator actions
  • External systems, contracts, and ownership
  • Failure points that damage throughput
02 / Connect

Connect the edges

API integrations are rarely just endpoints. They are contracts, credentials, retries, signatures, and data that needs to arrive in the right shape at the right point in the workflow.

  • OAuth, JWT, certificates, and signing
  • ERP and workflow systems
  • Reliable handoffs across internal modules
03 / Modernize

Modernize without stalling the business

When legacy systems block delivery, I use AI-assisted analysis and disciplined migration planning to move toward a stronger platform without pretending a rewrite solves everything.

  • Rails to .NET and Access to Blazor migrations
  • Incremental replacement of fragile modules
  • Continuity for users already inside the system
04 / Surface

Ship the interface people actually trust

The last mile still matters. Planning screens, dashboards, and desktop tools have to read well under pressure and support the actual rhythm of work, not a generic component showcase.

  • Web and desktop applications for daily operations
  • Readable interfaces for dense operational data
  • Iteration with consultants and support requests
